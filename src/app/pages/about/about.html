OUR STORY

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Kerala, Chest Day started with a simple mission: to provide a no-nonsense training ground for serious athletes. What began as a single garage gym has now grown into a fitness revolution across South India.

We believe in iron, sweat, and discipline. No shortcuts, just hard work. Our facilities are designed to push you to your limits and help you achieve the physique you've always dreamed of.
